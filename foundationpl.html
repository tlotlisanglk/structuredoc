Week 1: Foundation

Learning Objectives:
1. Introduction to UniPod Digital Fabrication Program
2. Version Control
3. Design Thinking
4. Computer-Aided Design

What I Achieved:
1. I learned what is FabLab and its importance of promoting innovation not only in Africa but across the globe. Also, how it will offer important training to us.
2. I used github for a long time and version control page became a refresher to the already existing knowledge I gained over the past years of my career.
3. Design thinking is the ability to carefully understand the user requirements and from there formulate the specifications based on the requirements from the user. There are different methods of designs used and one of them is Agile or Iterative design approach. This method allows the requirements to be adjusted during the design and implementation phases untill the user requirements are fully met.
4. The knowledge I already had in my past career about CAD softwares expanded even more when I was introduced to new CAD softwares listed on the Computer-Aided Design page. These CAD categories are Vector Graphics, Raster Graphics, 3D Design and Simulation.
